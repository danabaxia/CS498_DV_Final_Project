This slide is a fun reading for the food lovers and travellers. The purpose of this visualization is to explore the most popular food nationwide and regionwide. About the Visualization

About the Visualization

Interactive Slideshow This narrative visualization is presented as an interactive slideshow structure to guide users through differrent steps to the final conlcusion. User can click the arrow to flip between the slides.

Scenes This visualization consists of three scenes, following the same template of visualizing the data based on the geography, bar chart, bubule chart, providing a very consistent information display.

Annotations Anotation is used in chart 1 of the scene 1, to emphasize the ranking order starting from top popular food, also draw viewers attention to focus on the first several top ranking food.

Parameters Chart 2 provides the parameters of reviews count and allow the user to explore the data of intersting states. Chart 3 provides the parameters of the review score based on parameters of states and food category, so that the user can find out the best review score by state and food type.

Triggers Chart 3 allows the user to filter out the review score based on the food type. With different food category, the buble chart can be updated based on the updates of the filtered data.

The Number of Open Resturants by Category in USA

This bar chart shows the number of the food places classfied by the food type. We can easily find out the top most popular food places that are open in USA are Coffee shop, Pizza and brunch. Those are three top choice of Americans going out for eating

The Visiting Popuarity by States

This map is create to show the popularity of the food places across the country. The darker color means higher visits number and Yelp reviews. As we can see, California, Florida, New York are some of the darker area where more Resturants are present. It reflects the impact of tourism indrusty on resturants bussiness.
